# Architecture Decision Record Review Checklist

Use this checklist while executing the skill.

## Preparation

- [ ] Confirm decision topic, context, constraints, options considered, and stakeholders.
- [ ] Confirm technical requirements, non-goals, risks, and timelines.
- [ ] Confirm evidence such as prototypes, incidents, performance data, or team constraints.
- [ ] Confirm aDR format or repository documentation convention.

## Execution

- [ ] Clarify the decision being made and separate it from implementation tasks.
- [ ] Document context, constraints, options, evaluation criteria, and non-goals.
- [ ] State the decision directly and explain why rejected alternatives were not chosen.
- [ ] Capture consequences, risks, rollout plan, and review triggers.
- [ ] Keep the ADR factual, dated, and easy to update when circumstances change.

## Completion

- [ ] Future readers understand why the decision made sense at the time.
- [ ] Tradeoffs are explicit rather than hidden in implementation details.
- [ ] Alternatives are represented fairly.
- [ ] The ADR is short enough that the team will maintain it.
