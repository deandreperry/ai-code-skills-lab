# Onboarding Flow Review Review Checklist

Use this checklist while executing the skill.

## Preparation

- [ ] Confirm onboarding screens, routes, prototype, analytics, or screenshots.
- [ ] Confirm activation goal, primary user segment, and success metric.
- [ ] Confirm required setup steps, optional steps, and data dependencies.
- [ ] Confirm known drop-off points, support feedback, or usability findings.

## Execution

- [ ] Map the onboarding journey from entry point to first meaningful value.
- [ ] Separate required decisions from optional personalization and education.
- [ ] Review copy, progress indication, defaults, permissions, error recovery, and escape paths.
- [ ] Identify friction, premature complexity, trust gaps, accessibility issues, and missing states.
- [ ] Recommend sequence, content, interaction, and instrumentation improvements.

## Completion

- [ ] Users can reach first value with minimal confusion and unnecessary work.
- [ ] The flow explains why information or permissions are needed.
- [ ] Required and optional steps are clearly distinguished.
- [ ] Recommendations are testable and feasible for product and engineering teams.
