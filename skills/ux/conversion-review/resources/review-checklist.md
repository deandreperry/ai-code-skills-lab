# Conversion Review Review Checklist

Use this checklist while executing the skill.

## Preparation

- [ ] Confirm target conversion flow, screenshots, analytics, funnel data, or URL.
- [ ] Confirm audience, offer, conversion goal, and traffic source.
- [ ] Confirm known objections, support feedback, and competitive context.
- [ ] Confirm constraints around pricing, legal copy, analytics, and experimentation.

## Execution

- [ ] Clarify the desired conversion, user intent, promise, and decision moments.
- [ ] Review message clarity, information scent, trust cues, objection handling, form friction, pricing comprehension, and error recovery.
- [ ] Identify where users may hesitate, misunderstand, or abandon.
- [ ] Prioritize improvements by likely impact, implementation effort, and measurement quality.
- [ ] Recommend experiments, copy changes, UX fixes, and analytics events.

## Completion

- [ ] Recommendations are tied to conversion behavior and user decision-making.
- [ ] The review balances persuasion with accessibility and trust.
- [ ] The team receives testable hypotheses rather than vague growth advice.
- [ ] High-risk changes include measurement plans.
