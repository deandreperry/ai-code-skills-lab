# Conversion Review

## Skill Name

Conversion Review

## Description

Review conversion-critical experiences for clarity, trust, friction, persuasion, form effort, content, and measurement readiness.

## When To Use

- A signup, checkout, upgrade, demo request, or pricing flow underperforms.
- A team needs actionable improvements without a full growth experiment program.
- Stakeholders want to identify friction before launching a campaign.
- A flow needs better trust, value communication, or step completion.

## Inputs Required

- Target conversion flow, screenshots, analytics, funnel data, or URL.
- Audience, offer, conversion goal, and traffic source.
- Known objections, support feedback, and competitive context.
- Constraints around pricing, legal copy, analytics, and experimentation.

## Step-by-Step Workflow

1. Clarify the desired conversion, user intent, promise, and decision moments.
2. Review message clarity, information scent, trust cues, objection handling, form friction, pricing comprehension, and error recovery.
3. Identify where users may hesitate, misunderstand, or abandon.
4. Prioritize improvements by likely impact, implementation effort, and measurement quality.
5. Recommend experiments, copy changes, UX fixes, and analytics events.

## Output Format

- Conversion review with prioritized friction points and recommendations.
- Experiment ideas with hypothesis, target metric, and implementation notes.
- Analytics and instrumentation gaps.

## Success Criteria

- Recommendations are tied to conversion behavior and user decision-making.
- The review balances persuasion with accessibility and trust.
- The team receives testable hypotheses rather than vague growth advice.
- High-risk changes include measurement plans.

## Common Mistakes

- Optimizing only button color or superficial visual changes.
- Increasing urgency at the cost of trust or accessibility.
- Ignoring traffic source and user intent.
- Making claims that legal, brand, or product cannot support.

## Example Usage

Use this skill to review a SaaS pricing and trial signup flow for clarity, plan comparison, objections, field friction, and analytics gaps.
