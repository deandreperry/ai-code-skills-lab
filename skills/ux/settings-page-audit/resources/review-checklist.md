# Settings Page Audit Review Checklist

Use this checklist while executing the skill.

## Preparation

- [ ] Confirm settings routes, screenshots, sitemap, component code, or product requirements.
- [ ] Confirm user roles, permissions, and ownership boundaries.
- [ ] Confirm list of settings, destructive actions, and confirmation requirements.
- [ ] Confirm known support tickets, search terms, or usability concerns.

## Execution

- [ ] Inventory settings by domain, role, frequency, and risk.
- [ ] Review grouping, labels, search, navigation, save behavior, defaults, and unsaved-change handling.
- [ ] Check destructive actions, permissions, confirmations, auditability, and error recovery.
- [ ] Evaluate form accessibility, validation, loading states, and success feedback.
- [ ] Recommend IA, copy, interaction, and engineering changes in priority order.

## Completion

- [ ] Users can find and change settings without guessing or losing work.
- [ ] Dangerous actions are protected without blocking normal workflows.
- [ ] Permissions and ownership are clear.
- [ ] Save, success, failure, and unsaved-change states are predictable.
