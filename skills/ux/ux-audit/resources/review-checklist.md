# UX Audit Review Checklist

Use this checklist while executing the skill.

## Preparation

- [ ] Confirm target flow, screenshots, prototype, URL, or product description.
- [ ] Confirm primary users, goals, constraints, and success metrics.
- [ ] Confirm known pain points, analytics, support tickets, or usability feedback.
- [ ] Confirm design system and accessibility requirements.

## Execution

- [ ] Define the primary user goal, entry points, completion criteria, and constraints.
- [ ] Review the flow for clarity, hierarchy, affordance, feedback, errors, accessibility, responsiveness, and content quality.
- [ ] Distinguish usability blockers from polish issues and design preferences.
- [ ] Prioritize findings by impact on task completion and confidence in evidence.
- [ ] Recommend incremental improvements with product and engineering implications.

## Completion

- [ ] Findings explain how users are affected, not just what looks imperfect.
- [ ] Recommendations are specific and feasible within product constraints.
- [ ] Accessibility and content quality are included in UX judgment.
- [ ] The team can choose a first improvement without a redesign mandate.
