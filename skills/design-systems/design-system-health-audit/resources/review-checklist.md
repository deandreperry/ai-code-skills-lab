# Design System Health Audit Review Checklist

Use this checklist while executing the skill.

## Preparation

- [ ] Confirm component library source, docs, token files, usage data, and consumer feedback.
- [ ] Confirm release process, contribution model, ownership, and support channels.
- [ ] Confirm known bugs, adoption blockers, and cross-team dependencies.
- [ ] Confirm target platforms and product areas.

## Execution

- [ ] Review coverage, adoption, quality, accessibility, theming, documentation, tests, release process, and governance.
- [ ] Compare component APIs and token layers for consistency and duplication.
- [ ] Identify trust gaps such as missing states, unstable APIs, weak docs, or poor support.
- [ ] Prioritize improvements by product impact, team effort, and adoption leverage.
- [ ] Produce a roadmap that balances foundations, component gaps, and enablement work.

## Completion

- [ ] The audit explains why teams do or do not adopt the system.
- [ ] Recommendations balance technical quality with operating model improvements.
- [ ] The roadmap is credible for both engineers and design leadership.
- [ ] Findings are actionable without requiring a full restart.
